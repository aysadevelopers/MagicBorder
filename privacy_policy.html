Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Magic Border is developed by the AYSA Developer Team. Your privacy is very important to us. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Collection

Magic Border does not collect, track, or transmit any data about you, your device, or your usage. The app runs fully offline and does not connect to the internet or external servers.

Permissions Used

  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW – Used only to display the border overlay that the user enables manually.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE – Required to keep the border visible while you use other apps. The foreground service runs transparently with an ongoing notification.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Used only to show the persistent notification allowing the user to stop or hide the overlay.

All permissions are used solely for visual functionality within the app and do not involve any form of user data access, logging, or transmission.

Security

Since Magic Border operates entirely offline and performs only local visual rendering, no user information leaves your device at any time.

Children’s Privacy

This app is safe for all ages and does not collect or share any information about users, including children.
